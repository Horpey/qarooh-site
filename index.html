#whyTaxiTopoverBRT

1. Campaign Coverage and Peneration

BRT offers a linear transportation media that only moves on a pre-design route back and forth on the BRT lane, hence limiting the potential of audience reach.

Our Taxitops on the other hand, moves in a multi-dimensional route defined by the audience. This gives the client an unprecedented coverage, reach and penetration because it can move to any location, any estate, any CBD, any mall e.t.c

#whyTaxiTopoverBRT

2. Backlit display for an impactful night visibility

The BRT platform due to the nature of its implementation does not have lighting and hence it means clients do not get value for money in the night as it will be almost invisible to see the campaign on it .

Our Taxitop on the other hand, is a premium backlit media platform that lights up brightly in the night hence increases campaign visibility and impact even at night

#whyTaxiTopoverBRT

3. Longer Operational hours

BRTs are mandated by the government to operate within 6am to 9pm daily which means that the clients cant reach any more audience after 9pm or before 6am. .

Our Taxitop on the other hand has no operational time restrictions and hence operates 24hours which means that you get better campaign coverage morning / afternoon / night. They go to clubs, churches, cinemas, events and parties late into the night e.t.c
